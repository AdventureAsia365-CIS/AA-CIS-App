# Funnel Content (TOFU → MOFU → BOFU)

Funnel content maps messages to the buyer’s stage. TOFU builds awareness, MOFU builds trust and consideration, and BOFU drives conversion. A strong funnel does not use the same message for every audience.

## 5 persuasive funnel models

### 1. Classic 3-stage funnel

TOFU → MOFU → BOFU.

TOFU educates and attracts. MOFU deepens trust and comparison. BOFU gives proof, offer, and action.

### 2. 5-stage funnel

Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Intent → Decision.

Use for more complex customer journeys.

### 3. Reverse Funnel

Start with BOFU for hot audiences. Useful when the audience already knows the brand or has shown buying intent.

### 4. Hourglass Funnel

Adds loyalty after purchase: conversion → onboarding → retention → referral → repeat purchase.

### 5. Microfunnel

Build a full funnel inside one post, video, or landing section: hook → educate → proof → offer → CTA.

## 7 core techniques in funnel content

### 1. Content Type Mapping per Stage

TOFU: education, myth-busting, trends, stories. MOFU: comparisons, frameworks, case studies. BOFU: offers, demos, testimonials, pricing, urgency.

### 2. Psychological Trigger per Stage

TOFU uses curiosity and relevance. MOFU uses authority and social proof. BOFU uses risk reversal, urgency, and specificity.

### 3. Lead Magnet Bridge

Move TOFU readers into MOFU with a useful download, checklist, quiz, guide, webinar, or report.

### 4. Nurture Sequence

Use repeated value to build trust before asking for purchase.

### 5. BOFU Triple-Stack

At BOFU, combine offer clarity, proof, and risk reversal.

### 6. Retargeting Loop

Use retargeting content to answer objections and reframe value for warm audiences.

### 7. Loyalty Content

After purchase, create onboarding, usage tips, success stories, referral prompts, and community content.

## 8-point checklist before publishing funnel content

1. What stage is this content for?
2. Is the CTA appropriate for that stage?
3. Does the message match audience awareness?
4. Is there a bridge to the next stage?
5. Is proof used at the right moment?
6. Are objections addressed before conversion?
7. Is there a post-purchase plan?
8. Are channels and formats mapped clearly?

## Mistakes to avoid

1. Selling too hard at TOFU.
2. Educating too much at BOFU.
3. No bridge between stages.
4. Same CTA for every audience.
5. No retargeting or nurture logic.
6. Forgetting loyalty after purchase.

## Recommended psychology by stage

TOFU: curiosity, identity, novelty. MOFU: authority, social proof, specificity. BOFU: loss aversion, risk reversal, scarcity, commitment. Loyalty: belonging, reciprocity, consistency.

## Recommended NLP by stage

TOFU: open loops and pattern interrupt. MOFU: explanation frames and belief shift. BOFU: embedded commands and risk reversal. Loyalty: identity reinforcement and future pacing.
