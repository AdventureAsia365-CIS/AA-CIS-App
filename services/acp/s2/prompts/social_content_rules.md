# Social Content Rules

## Purpose

This file guides the creation of social media content ideas for Adventure Asia.

Social content should help people notice, trust, and explore Adventure Asia tours.

It should support the Tour Visibility Engine.

Social content should connect to:

- blog content
- FAQ content
- destination insights
- activity themes
- live AA tour pages

---

## Main Goal

Create social content ideas that help the right travellers discover Adventure Asia tours.

Social content should create:

- curiosity
- trust
- destination awareness
- activity awareness
- soft-adventure positioning
- traffic to blog or tour pages

---

## Adventure Asia Positioning

Adventure Asia is a premium curated soft-adventure brand.

Adventure Asia is not:

- backpacker group travel
- budget adventure travel
- mass-market package tours
- ultra-luxury excess

Adventure Asia serves experienced professionals and higher-income travellers who want:

- cultural depth
- active but comfortable travel
- smooth logistics
- expert local planning
- private-feeling journeys
- curated adventure across Asia
- local knowledge without chaos
- adventure with quality and confidence

---

## Social Platforms

Create ideas for:

- Facebook
- Instagram
- TikTok
- LinkedIn
- YouTube Shorts

Not every idea needs every platform.

Choose the best platform based on topic.

---

## Required Fields for Each Social Idea

Each social idea should include:

- platform
- post type
- hook
- message
- destination
- activity
- related blog title if available
- related AA tour title
- related AA tour URL
- CTA
- priority score
- notes

---

## Post Types

Use post types such as:

- short reel
- carousel
- image post
- founder insight
- destination insight
- travel planning tip
- FAQ post
- route explainer
- soft-adventure explainer
- blog teaser
- tour teaser
- LinkedIn thought post
- YouTube Shorts script

---

## Platform Guidance

### Instagram

Best for:

- visual destination inspiration
- carousels
- reels
- route highlights
- “what to expect” posts

Tone:

- refined
- visual
- calm
- curious

### TikTok

Best for:

- short travel insight
- surprising destination facts
- quick planning tips
- route ideas
- myth-busting

Tone:

- natural
- direct
- human
- not childish
- not noisy

### Facebook

Best for:

- longer destination captions
- blog sharing
- community discussion
- travel planning posts
- tour discovery

Tone:

- warm
- informative
- practical

### LinkedIn

Best for:

- premium traveller positioning
- founder perspective
- travel trend insight
- business traveller / executive lifestyle angle
- market insight

Tone:

- thoughtful
- strategic
- calm
- polished

### YouTube Shorts

Best for:

- quick destination explainers
- route previews
- “why this country works for soft adventure”
- FAQ answers

Tone:

- clear
- concise
- useful

---

## Hook Rules

Hooks should be calm but interesting.

Good examples:

- Taiwan is becoming one of Asia’s most interesting cycling destinations.
- Nepal is not only for extreme trekkers.
- Laos rewards travellers who prefer depth over speed.
- Japan walking journeys are about more than scenery.
- Vietnam works beautifully for active travellers who still want comfort.

Avoid:

- You won’t believe this
- This will blow your mind
- Cheapest tour in Asia
- Insane adventure
- Hidden gem everyone must visit
- Book now before it’s gone
- Backpacker-style hype

---

## Message Rules

The message should be:

- short
- clear
- useful
- premium
- human
- connected to a real travel decision

It should explain why the destination or activity matters.

It should not be generic inspiration.

Bad:

```text
Discover the magic of Asia with unforgettable adventures.
