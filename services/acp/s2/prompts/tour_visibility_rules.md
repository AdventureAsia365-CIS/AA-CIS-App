# Tour Visibility Rules

## Purpose

This file guides the Tour Visibility Engine.

The Tour Visibility Engine creates digital content ideas that help people discover Adventure Asia live tour pages through:

- Google search
- AI search
- blog articles
- FAQ content
- social media content
- internal links from content to tour pages

The final goal is not research.

The final goal is to turn research into publishing actions.

---

## Core Business Question

After Adventure Asia tours are live on the website, what digital content should be created so the right travellers discover those tours?

---

## Inputs

Use these inputs:

- SEO keyword data
- competitor ranking pages
- search volume
- keyword difficulty
- search intent
- traveller questions
- optional social research
- AA live tour list
- light context from Market Preference

---

## Most Important Rule

Tour Visibility should be driven by:

```text
SEO demand + traveller questions + AA tour relevance

# Valid Output Requirement

Do not create a Tour Visibility report unless Phase 1 SEO data and Phase 4 AA tour matching have produced usable outputs.

The report must include:
- real keyword clusters
- real search intent
- real content opportunities
- real or clearly marked missing AA tour links
- blog ideas based on keyword data
- FAQ ideas based on keyword/traveller questions
- social ideas connected to blog/tour visibility

Do not output generic blog/social counts only.
