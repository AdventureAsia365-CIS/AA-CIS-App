# Blog Brief Rules

## Purpose

This file guides the creation of blog content briefs for Adventure Asia.

The blog briefs should help Adventure Asia create content that brings the right travellers to live tour pages.

The blog is not the final destination.

The blog should guide the reader toward a relevant Adventure Asia tour page.

---

## Main Goal

Create blog ideas that improve:

- Google search visibility
- AI search visibility
- destination authority
- activity authority
- internal linking to AA tours
- trust with premium soft-adventure travellers

---

## Inputs

Use:

- SEO keyword data
- competitor ranking pages
- traveller questions
- optional social research
- AA tour matches
- Market Preference context, lightly

---

## Core Rule

Do not copy competitor content.

Do not rewrite competitor tour pages.

Do not create a blog only because a competitor sells a tour.

Create a blog when:

1. there is search demand,
2. the topic fits Adventure Asia positioning,
3. the blog can answer useful traveller questions,
4. the blog can naturally link to AA live tours.

---

## Adventure Asia Positioning

Adventure Asia is a premium curated soft-adventure brand.

Adventure Asia is not:

- backpacker group travel
- budget adventure travel
- mass-market package tours
- ultra-luxury excess

Adventure Asia serves experienced professionals and higher-income travellers who want:

- cultural depth
- active but comfortable travel
- smooth logistics
- expert local planning
- private-feeling journeys
- curated adventure across Asia
- local knowledge without chaos
- adventure with quality and confidence

---

## Blog Brief Output

Each blog brief must include:

- blog title
- primary keyword
- secondary keywords
- search intent
- target reader
- article angle
- why this topic matters
- suggested outline
- recommended AA tour links
- suggested anchor text
- CTA
- FAQ questions
- AI-search-ready summary block
- priority score
- notes for writer

---

## Blog Title Rules

Blog titles should be clear, useful, and premium.

Good examples:

- Taiwan Cycling Holidays: Routes, Seasons, and What to Expect
- Best Soft Adventure Experiences in Laos for Curious Travellers
- Nepal Trekking with Comfort: What Experienced Travellers Should Know
- Japan Walking Tours: Culture, Nature, and Local Depth
- How to Choose an Active Holiday in Vietnam Without Joining a Mass Group Tour

Avoid:

- Cheap Taiwan Tours
- Ultimate Bucket List Adventure
- 10 Insane Things to Do in Nepal
- Best Tour Ever
- Adventure Asia vs Intrepid
- Why Our Tour Is Better Than Competitors

---

## Search Intent Categories

Classify each blog idea by intent:

- informational
- commercial research
- comparison
- destination planning
- activity planning
- seasonal planning
- safety/logistics
- itinerary research

Commercial research and destination planning are usually high value.

---

## Article Angle Rules

The article angle should reflect AA positioning.

Use angles like:

- active but comfortable travel
- cultural depth
- expert local planning
- smoother logistics
- private-feeling journey
- premium soft adventure
- local knowledge
- meaningful travel
- avoiding mass-market travel without needing ultra-luxury

Do not use angles like:

- cheapest trip
- backpacker route
- extreme adventure
- luxury excess
- party travel
- mass group tour comparison attack

---

Each blog brief should include a useful outline.

Recommended structure:

```md

## What kind of traveller this suits
